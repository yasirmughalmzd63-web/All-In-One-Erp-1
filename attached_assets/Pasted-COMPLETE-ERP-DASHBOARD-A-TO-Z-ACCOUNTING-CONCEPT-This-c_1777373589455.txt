COMPLETE ERP DASHBOARD — A TO Z ACCOUNTING CONCEPT

This covers Stock, Sales, Purchase, Cash, Accounts, Audit, and Final Reports in one integrated dashboard.

1) CORE ACCOUNTING FLOW (How System Works)

Purchase → Stock → Sale → Cash Collection → Expense → Profit → Reports

This is the main business cycle.

Example Flow:

Supplier → Purchase Stock
Stock → Sell Product
Customer → Pay Cash
Cash → Deposit to Company Account
System → Calculate Profit

3) DAILY TRANSACTIONS MODULE

This is where all accounting starts.

Transactions Types
Sales

When product is sold.

System must:

Increase Sales
Decrease Stock
Increase Cash

Purchase

When stock is received.

System must:

Increase Stock
Increase Payable or Decrease Cash

Expense

When money is spent.

System must:

Decrease Cash
Increase Expense

Payment Received

When customer pays.

System must:

Decrease Receivable
Increase Cash

Payment Paid

When company pays supplier.

System must:

Decrease Cash
Decrease Payable
